# Raw Idea

## Overview

Mailchimp Clone is a modern marketing team platform focused on the core campaign lifecycle for agencies managing multiple client audiences.  
v1-lite aims for balanced, shallow coverage across key modules rather than deep specialization in one area.

## Initial Problem

Small-to-mid agencies often manage campaigns across fragmented tools and inconsistent processes.  
This creates operational friction in audience management, campaign execution, and performance visibility.

## Potential Users

- Agencies managing multiple client audiences
- Marketing operators running repeated campaign cycles per client
- Campaign managers needing reusable templates and basic reporting

## Why This Idea Exists

- Agencies need one practical workspace for campaign creation, scheduling, and tracking
- Reusable templates and segmentation should reduce repetitive setup work
- Teams need basic performance visibility without enterprise-level analytics overhead

## Initial Constraints

- Spec-first incubation workflow; no implementation-first decisions yet
- v1-lite should remain balanced but intentionally shallow across major modules
- Must stay technology-agnostic outside architecture notes
- Avoid enterprise complexity and premature scale assumptions

### v1-lite Success Statement

After 30 days, the team should reliably be able to create, manage, schedule, and track marketing email campaigns for segmented audiences using reusable templates and basic analytics.

### Explicitly Out of Scope (v1-lite)

- advanced automation workflows
- AI-generated campaigns
- multichannel messaging
- SMS marketing
- realtime collaboration
- enterprise deliverability tooling
- A/B testing
- advanced analytics
- approval workflows
- role-based permissions
- external integrations

## Unknowns

- Client/account model shape for multi-client agency operations in v1-lite
- Campaign template editing depth for v1-lite (simple editor vs richer content tools)
