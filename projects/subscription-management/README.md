## Current v1-lite Position

- Project readiness: `spec-defined`
- Scope posture: solo-first subscription visibility and tracking
- Persistence direction: local-first with migration-ready data contracts
- Major remaining open decisions:
  - renewal reminder strictness
  - review cadence policy

## Working Rules

- Keep workflow spec-first, not implementation-first.
- Lock decisions incrementally and update relevant specs immediately.
- Avoid scope creep into full personal-finance platform behavior.
